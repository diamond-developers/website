By: Era

So. What is the quality level exactly?

Musical uses a HighWaterMark of 80 > 120.
The reason why Musical may experience a little bit of lag during playing, is because HighWaterMark determines how much to buffer, and since it pretty high, it may experience lag.

Will this be increased?

When we attempt to increase the HighWaterMark, we have to factor in the folowing

  • Connection speeds,
  • Latency increase,
  • Cloud Speeds.

So when we increase it, it may effect everything about Musical.
In other words, we can not increase until we are in the position to change our cloud hosting.
